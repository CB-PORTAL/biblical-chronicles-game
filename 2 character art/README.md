## Sprite Specifications
- **Size**: 64x64 pixels (standard), 128x128 (detailed)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Colors**: Limited palette for retro feel
- **States**: At least 3 per major character
- **Naming**: `charactername_state.png`
